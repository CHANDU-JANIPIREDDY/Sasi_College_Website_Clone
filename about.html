SASI Institute of Technology & Engineering

SASI Institute of Technology & Engineering started its functioning with four academic programs like CSE, ECE, EEE & ME. Now the Institute offers twelve UG programs (CE, CSE, CST, ECE, ECT, EEE, ME, IT, CSM, CSD, CIC & AIML); Five PG programs (CSE, PS, VLSI &ES, CAD/CAM, & MBA), Five Research programs (ECE, CSE, EEE, ME & Physics) and Four Diploma programs (ECE, EEE, ME & CME). It created a sensation in the field of engineering education by receiving NAAC, NBA accreditations, 2(f) & 12(B) status, permanent affiliation from JNTUK.

NAAC- A+

SASI Institute of Technology & Engineering (SITE) has been awarded the prestigious NAAC A+ accreditation, a testament to its excellence in academic standards, infrastructure, and holistic development of students. This recognition reflects the institute's commitment to delivering quality education and fostering innovation.

About College

SASI has created its own brand image for the values it follows. SASI educational society with its strong roots in west and east godavari districts is imparting quality education for over 40+ years. The total students strength of all the institutions run by the society is about 50,000+ what began as a small school.
